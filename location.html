Choosing the right dam location

When choosing location for the dam, many factors have to be taken into account. Our dam is located on the Acharistskali river between villages Didachara and Iakobadzebi where the flow rate is 20m3/s and we have a route towards our turbine, with another river acting as a failsafe detour.

This setup is safe and scalable, as we planned out the setup of additional turbines on demand to increase power output.

The river is surrounded with mountains, which eliminates the risk of flooding on a large area in case the dam fails. The turbine is located next to the town of Khula with the tunnel going straight trough the mountain. This allows quick power transmission.


Why is a dam already there?

Our team actually has no idea. It wasn't there on our maps when we designed it, so maybe we could take it down?


Explaining the choice

Not only a flow of 20m3/s is more than enough to provide irrigation services to the nearby farmers, it allows the turbine to run with a higher output and consistency.
