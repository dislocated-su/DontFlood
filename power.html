Hydropower project

Turbine

When we decided to have our turbine close to the dam, we were thinking about the concept of expandability. It is possible to put more turbines on a greater distance to generate more power.

So, since it is close, and we have a town of Khulo close to us, let's build a turbine right next to them! That way we can connect the city and upgrade it's power lines while connecting the turbine to the grid.

The turbine we chose was the Francis because it is the most efficient turbine for this environment, particularly potential head and flow rate.


Power calculations

To calculate power output of the turbine, we just need to use simple equation: $P=ρgHQη$


Where:

$ρ = 100kg/m^3$ - Water density

$g = 9.81m/s^2$ - Effect of gravity

$H = 270m$ - Net potential head

$Q = 20m^3/s$ - Flow rate

$η = 90$ - Efficiency

Which brings us to 47676.6 kW produced. Convert to kWh $47676.6 kW * 8760h * 0.6 = 250588209.6 kWh$


Explaining the choice

Not only a flow of 20m 3/s is more than enough to provide irrigation services to the nearby farmers, it allows the turbine to run with a higher output and consistency.
