Even more benefits

Recreational use

Dams provide prime recreational facilities throughout the World. Boating, camping, picnic areas, and boat launch facilities are all supported by dams. After the construction has finished, the dam can attract people with recreational activities. This can result in nearby city growth or new territory adopted.

Cheap power

If the hydroelectric power station is going to be connected to the National Grid of Georgia, electricity rates are expected to go down, which will increase population wealth. That contribution is going to benefit everyone, regardless of whenever the power supplied is from this station or not.

Handling emmisions

Large amounts of carbon tied up in trees and other plants are released when the reservoir is initially flooded and the plants rot. These rotten plants decompose without oxygen and produce methane. To solve this, local timber initiative will be launched and during the constuction the trees and plants will be sold.

Managing impacts

During the process of planning we had to take into account environmental impacts the hydropower soulution could cause, and have successfully developed a solution for all of them. We can truly state that this hydropower project is safe to the environment and definately benefit everyone.

Boost to the economy

As our dam is in construction it will provide a range of economic, social and environmental benefits. We will provide thousands of jobs to locals to a 50:50 ratio with trained professionals which will grant extra perks including: Gaining knowledge, developing key skills, securing a graduate job and more. The community will also benefit from this project.

Expansion possibilites

It is possible to install more turbines on demand. The new turbines can be located at the same place, or further apart to increase power generation. This brings new possibilites to Georgian government in terms of managing power production. A long-term solution to any country's power problem.
