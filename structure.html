Dam specs

Our engineering team has been working hard to figure out the best possible parameters for the dam. Here's the result:

Height

100m

Width

175m

Foundation depth

4m

Angle of internal friction

36°

This dam uses the force of gravity to balance the pushing force of water, so it's main material is going to be concrete, a heavy material. Our engineering team wanted the dam to hold a generous amount of water, so the dam is reinforced with steel bars.


Design

The dam has a straight shape, and a pathway connecting two sides of the river, and flood things.

Foundation

We analysed soil by taking SPT analysis and concluded that foundation will be 4 metres deep, since this value is perfect balance between reliability and expense. Anything below 4 metres would be much more expensive and would not affect the reliability of the structure.
